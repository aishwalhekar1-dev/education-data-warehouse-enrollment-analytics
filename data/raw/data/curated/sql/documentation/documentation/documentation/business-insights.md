# Business Insights Summary

## Enrollment Trends
- Enrollment shows consistent growth across academic terms, indicating increased demand for education programs.
- Certain programs demonstrate higher enrollment concentration, suggesting areas for resource prioritization.

## Funding Analysis
- Funding distribution varies significantly by program, highlighting opportunities for budget reallocation.
- Some students receive higher funding amounts due to program structure and credit load.

## Data Quality Impact
- Enforcing data quality rules eliminated incomplete student records and invalid enrollments.
- Standardization of academic terms improved reporting accuracy and trend analysis.

## Business Value
- Enables leadership to make informed decisions on staffing, budgeting, and program planning.
- Improves transparency and audit readiness for education and government reporting.
